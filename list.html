Training Materials

Training materials such as online e-learning courses, scoping and desk studies, power points, manuals, SOPs and guidelines are available in this section.

The IPPC Secratariat encourages all IPPC Contracting Parties, RPPOs and other relevant organizations to contribute by uploading training materials and/or link to available online e-learning courses. These will be crucial resources for the improvement of Phytosanitary Capacity Development around the world.
