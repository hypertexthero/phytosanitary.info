Find experts in topics related to plant protection and phytosanitary measures.

This database of service providers on phytosanitary issues has details of individuals and entities with thematic experience and competencies relevant to developing phytosanitary capacity including scientific, managerial and legal expertise.

Experts in topics related to plant protection, phytosanitary measures and capacity development are invited to complete the application form and upload CVs so your profile (if approved) can be listed in the roster.
