Pest Risk Analysis: Ensuring Food Security

Pest risk analysis helps ensure food security by providing a mechanism for countries to import agricultural products while protecting their own food resources from the risk of exotic plants and diseases. Read More »

What is Pest Risk Analysys

PRA is the process of determining the risk posed by an exotic plant pest of concern by assessing the likelihood the pest will be introduced, estimating any associated consequences, and then, if necessary, identifying appropriate methods to reduce the risk. Read more »

Why is PRA Important?

The introduction and spread of exotic pestes and diseases cause huge impacts for countries each year. Countries tha invest in a strong pest risk analysis program are making a wise investment towards the protection of their agricultural and natural resources. Read more »

What do we need for PRA?

The implementation of a PRA program begins with having a trained, stable staff who are knowledgeable about science and risk assessment methods and have easy access to information. Many resources to help, whatever your stage of development. Read more »
