Meetings National Reporting Obligations in Central Africa

Training under an FAO sub-regional TCP for central African countries for national IPPC editors to meet their national reporting obligations.

Hands on training for IPPC national editors on national reporting obligations (NROs) and how this data needs to be submitted through the International Phytosanitary Portal (IPP - www.ippc.int). Lastly, participants ended the workshop by reviewing their NRO action plan and agreeing to actions for the next 12 months or more.

Expected Outputs
The immediate objective of this project is to enhance the capacity of developing country NPPOs to implement the IPPC and ISPMs by providing them with internationally accepted technical resources, such as manuals, operational procedures and training kits, developed and available for use in the management of a phytosanitary system covering areas such as Import regulatory system, Sampling of consignments, Export Certification, Pest Diagnostics, Pest Surveillance, PRA, etc.
