Market Access: Guide to phytosanitary issues for national plant protection organizations

The guide is geared towards NPPOs to provide information and context on phytosanitary aspects of market access negotiations. The primary purpose of this guide is to describe a process that can be followed to gain market access with the least hindrance to trade but, at the same time, preventing the introduction and spread of pests into new areas.

The guide covers:

  • Rights and obligations of the NPPOs as they apply to trade in plants and plant products.
  • A practical guide for achieving market access; and
  • Maintaining trade

Please also find the power point presentation to this Market Access manual in the phytosanitary page.
